Hi! I'm a Computer Science PhD student at UC Santa Cruz. I'm currently leading a team for the [2026 Amazon Nova AI Challenge](https://www.amazon.science/nova-ai-challenge/amazon-nova-ai-challenge-returns-with-nova-forge-access-for-competing-teams), where my proposal is one of the 10 selected to compete. Also, I recently led [ZeroDayBench](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02297) a cyberdefense coding eval for frontier AI models that was done through [Supervised Program for Alignment Research (SPAR)](https://sparai.org/). Our project also won the First Place Best Poster award ($2000) out of 80 projects. My work has been supported by GPU compute credits from Prime Intellect, Lium.io, and Modal.
