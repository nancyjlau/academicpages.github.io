Hi! I'm a Computer Science PhD student at UC Santa Cruz. I'm currently leading a team for the [2026 Amazon Nova AI Challenge](https://www.amazon.science/nova-ai-challenge/amazon-nova-ai-challenge-returns-with-nova-forge-access-for-competing-teams), where my proposal is one of the 10 selected to compete. Recently worked on [ZeroDayBench](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02297), a cyberdefense coding eval for frontier AI models that was done through [Supervised Program for Alignment Research (SPAR)](https://sparai.org/). It won the SPAR First Place Best Poster award ($2000), and accepted to [ICLR 2026 Workshop on Agents in the Wild](https://agentwild-workshop.github.io/). My work has been supported by GPU compute credits from Prime Intellect, Lium.io, and Modal.
